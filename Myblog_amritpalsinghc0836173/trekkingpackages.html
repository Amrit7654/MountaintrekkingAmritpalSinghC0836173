Beas Kund Trekking Tour Highlights

1.The trail offers several breathtaking sites that are ideal for nature photography

2.Witness the spectacular views of Indrasan, Hanuman Tibba and Deo Tibba Peak

3.Enjoy the solace at the Solang Nallah and the Manu Temple

Best Time to Visit Beas Kund Trek

The best time for Beas Kund Trek is between Mid-May and Mid-October. The weather remains quite salubrious making the trek easy and ofcourse thrilling.

2.Hampta Pass Trekking

Hampta Pass Trekking Tour Highlights

1.Witness the immaculate and picturesque alpine like Chandratal

2.Camp under the star-studded sky and enjoy delicious and nutritious meals everyday

3.Get the opportunity to capture the splendid views of the Pir Panjal Ranges

4.Find excitement in crossing some of the high mountain passes

Best Time to Visit Hampta Pass Trek

The best time to trek to Hampta Pass is from mid-June to mid-October. There are chances to witness some snow on the high passes in the early days of the season and then it melts down completely by August. Rain is expected during the monsoons in Kullu-Manali valley but in Spiti, the sun will be bright as usual. The temperature is likely to stay between 12 and 20 °C during the day, while at night, temperature at the highest campsite would be between -2 to 6 °C. In the latter part of the trekking season (Sep – mid-Oct) a bit of snowfall is a possibility. The temperatures during the day will be between 12-18 °C and the evenings will start getting cold with temperature dropping down from 4 to -6°C.

3.Kheerganga Trekking

Kheerganga Trekking Tour Highlights

1.Kheerganga is one of the unique Himalayan treks in which you are rewarded with excellent meadows, forested ways with pine trees, couple of cascades and crossing towns on reaching the summit at almost 10,000 feet

2.Know the secret by locals, why Lord Shiva's son Kartikey meditated here for thousand years

3.At Kheerganga, you’ll be rewarded with the breathtaking Parvati Kund - The holy hot water spring, just what you need to relax when the weather is cool

4.Give attention to the mix of Indian and Hippie culture at Kheerganga where you would be surprised to see the cross culture

5.Be ready to witness and indulge in landscape photography during your stay as the view of the sunset and trekking through Kheerganga forests are an incredible experience.

6.Do your mental therapy by camping under the starlit sky at the top of the Parvati Valley

Best Time to Visit Kheerganga Trek

Throughout the Year Day: 8°C to 15°C; and Night: 0°C to 3°C Kheerganga Trek Height: 9600 ft.

Day 01 New Delhi to Bhuntar | Journey to Hit the the Mini Israel of India Start your adventurous journey by boarding an evening Semi Sleeper AC Volvo bus/Tempo traveller/Sedan car/SUV car (depending on the group size) from Delhi around 05:00 PM Overnight stay in the bus.

Day 02 Kasol to Chalal excursion | Discover the ‘Little Amsterdam’ On reaching Bhuntar, take a breath of fresh air amidst views of picturesque hills and head to the campsite in kasol with the help of local transportation at your own expense. En route to Chalal, explore the ‘Little Amsterdam’, the lanes of Kasol lined with vibrant souvenir shops and Israeli cafes on your own After spending a wonderful day in Chalal, get back to your campsite in the evening Enjoy dancing around the bonfire, as you have a scrumptious dinner Overnight stay in Kasol.

Day 03 Kasol to Kheerganga | Capture a Whimsical dip in the Natural Hot Water Spring After breakfast, move to the Barshaini to start your exciting Kheerganga Trek. Barshaini is a small village near Kasol and the point of confluence of the Parvati and Tosh Rivers In the initial part of this Kheerganga trek, feast your eyes upon picturesque waterfalls like the majestic Rudra Nag, and take a small halt to admire the beauty of the landform Trek for around 14 km along the gushing Parvati River and pass through the tranquil villages of Kalga and Pulga After reaching the green meadow of Kheerganga, take a refreshing dip in the hot mythical Parvati Kund to wash off the fatigue of the journey Later, enjoy a bonfire near your camp to gather energy for the next day Overnight stay in Camps.

Day 04 Kheerganga to Kasol via Barshaini | Descend from the Majestic Land Wake up to a magnificent view of the Himalayan peaks, and prepare for the journey back to Barshaini through the same route you took to reach Kheerganga Reach Barshaini, and board transportation from here to Bhuntar at your own expense From Bhuntar, Head back to Delhi and say goodbye to the mountains.
